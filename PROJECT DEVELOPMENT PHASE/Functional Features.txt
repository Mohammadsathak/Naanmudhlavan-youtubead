Here are some of the functional features included in the solution for YouTube ad campaign about fashion clothes:

Target audience selection:
The ability to target ads to specific demographics, interests, and behaviors, such as people who have recently searched for fashion-related terms.

Ad format selection:
The ability to choose from a variety of ad formats, such as skippable in-stream ads, non-skippable in-stream ads, bumper ads, and sponsored cards.

Bidding strategy selection:
The ability to choose from a variety of bidding strategies, such as cost per click (CPC), cost per view (CPV), and cost per thousand impressions (CPM).

Ad creative optimization:
The ability to test and optimize ad creatives, such as different video lengths, thumbnails, and text, to improve campaign performance.

Reporting and analysis:
The ability to track and analyze campaign performance, such as impressions, clicks, views, and conversions.

These features can help fashion brands reach their target audience, engage with them, and drive sales.
